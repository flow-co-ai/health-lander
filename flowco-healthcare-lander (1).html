Common questions

The honest answers we'd give on a call anyway.

How is this different from hiring an agency? +
Most agencies hand you off to bloated account teams or chase retainers with no integration. We work as your internal department — specialists in their lanes, one strategy, accountable for the system end-to-end. The difference isn't the work; it's the operating model.
Do you guarantee a specific number of leads? +
We don't lead with lead-volume guarantees because that's how generalist agencies commodify themselves and end up competing on price. We guarantee the system — that what gets installed actually works as one connected engine. Volume and revenue compound from there. We'll cover what realistic numbers look like for your specific situation on the call.
What does this cost? +
It depends on what your practice needs and what stage you're at. We cover pricing on the strategy call once we've actually looked at your situation. Building pricing on a website before we know what you need would be the same lazy thinking we're trying to replace.
How long until we see results? +
Most practices see the first system improvements (CRM clean-up, follow-up gaps closed, reporting visibility) inside the first two to three weeks. Lead volume and revenue lift typically show up in months two and three as campaigns dial in and the system starts compounding.
What if we already have an agency or freelancers? +
Most of our clients did when they came to us. We start with a diagnostic. Sometimes we recommend keeping a piece of what you have. Sometimes we replace the whole stack. Either way, we're honest about it — we don't pretend a fragmented setup can be fixed when it can't.
I've been burned by marketing before. +
Almost everyone has. Usually it's one of three things: the strategy was generic and not customized, the service was slow and unsupported, or the operational layer was missing entirely. We'd rather diagnose what actually happened than ask you to take another leap of faith — the call is built around that conversation.
Do you only work with healthcare? +
We specialize in owner-led healthcare and local service businesses. Outside of those, we'd rather refer you to someone better positioned than be a generalist for you. The whole point of this model is that we're not generalists.
